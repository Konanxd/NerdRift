EXPLANATION

Runes

You have choice between Conqueror and Kraken Slayer for Keystone rune.

Conqueror will help you to sustain in fight because at the full stack, you gain 6 bonus AD and 5% omnivamp will help you to gain some lifesteal. This rune is good against some AD or AP champions when the enemies have a less tank or bruiser on their side. Meanwhile, Kraken Slayer is really good against 2+ tank or bruiser because of the true damage you will get for the third attack. True damage will ignore armor or magic resistant. Also, it will help you burst the squishy enemy since it has a true damage.

For Domination, you have three choices depends on your playstyle, Hunter - Vampirism, Brutal, or Giant Slayer.

If you want to scaling into the late game with 10 AD and have 5% physical vamp, you can choose Hunter - Vampirism. If you want to win your lane and early game, you can choose Brutal. If you meet 2+ tank or bruiser, you can choose Giant Slayer.

Same as Domination rune, Resolve rune is also depends on your playstyle. You can choose Hunter - Titan, Nullifying Orb, or Bone Plating.

Hunter - Titan helps you to get more health and tenacity so you don't have to concern to any crowd control. Nullifying Orb will help you survive from burst damage. Bone PLating will block damage and helps you survive in a 1v1 situation.

Manaflow Band is the best Inspiration rune for Ezreal.

Manaflow Band grants you maximum mana by hitting enemy up to 300 mana. By gains more maximum mana, you will also get more damage from Manamune since it scales with maximum mana.

Laning-Early Phase

Long Sword is the best starting items since you can get more damage for securing last hit. You can also take Sapphire Crystal for early stack of Mana Charge. However, you will have to play safely especially against early poking champions. Your next item for the first recall is going to be Tear of Goddess (if you bought Sapphire Crystal, you can buy Long Sword for extra damage for securing last hit. You can also skip it into Sheen for aggressive play.). This is the item you should prioritize because it will saving time for stacking the passive into Muramana. For the next item, you should get Sheen since it grants you 100% base AD bonus physical damage on-hit. If you against regen, healer, or lifestealer champion, you should buy Executioner's Calling for the anti-heal (Grievious Wound) effect so the enemy will get a less of HP healing.

After you bought Sheen, you can get Manamune for the mana sustain and damage that depends on your 1,5% maximum mana.

Mid Game

Trinity Force is the core item for Ezreal because it will increase your damage after using ability for 200% base AD bonus physical damage on-hit. Therefore, Trinity Force is the best pair for Ezreal, even better than Lux and MORE THAN Zoe (sorry shipper). He is relying on his 1st ability, Mystic Shot, and it applies to Trinity Force passive because it has low cooldown and you should abuse it.

Furthemore, Iceborn Gauntlet also can be the alternative of Trinity Force since it still give you the Sheen passive yet it only has 100% base AD bonus physical damage on-hit that scales with +25% bonus armor. However, it has icy zone that also can slow the enemies and deal a damage around it same as the bonus damage stat and the zone radius is depends on the +55% bonus armor.

If you in winning condition, you can buy and upgrade your boots into Ionian Boots of Lucidity for Ability Haste or Gluttonous Greaves for omnivamp. You can buy Ionian Boots of Lucidity when your build purpose will have enough lifesteal. However, when your build purpose will have lack of lifesteal, Gluttonous Greaves helps you to sustain in fight with the lifesteal that regaining your HP back quickly.

If you in losing condition due to having a 1v2 situation in the early game since your support don't even know how to play nor engage the fight and then he's throwing up by having a lot of death in the scoreboard or might be because of you don't know how to play properly and then everyone in the team got tilted because how the duo laner messed up their lane, you can skip the boots and do some farm until you get your Trinty Force. The game is not ended yet, so just keep farming for the recovery.

Serylda's Grudge is good as the next when you in winning condition and there is no regen, heal, or lifesteal champions since it has an Armor Penetration and Ability Haste. Even there is a tank or bruiser, rushing this item when in winning condition is a good choice.

When you matchup against 2+ tank or bruiser or in losing condition and having the same matchup, you can rush Blade of the Ruined King as the third item because the damage is scaling with the enemy HP.

You also can burst down the squishy enemy with Riftmaker since it has true damage passive when you in combat. Also, it grants you 15% omnivamp.

Facing against burst champion (especially AP champion) is really tough for Ezreal. Maw of Malmortius will prevent you from getting burst with the magic resistant and Lifeline effect.

For the boots upgrade, Stasis Enchant will helps your aggressive playstyle while Quicksilver Enchant will save you from a lot of crowd control.

Late Game

NOTE: Last Whisper CAN'T BE STACKED. YOU HAVE TO CHOOSE BETWEEN Serylda's Grudge AND Mortal Reminder.

You should upgrade your Executioner's Calling into Mortal Reminder when against regen, healer, or lifesteal champions. Besides the Armor Penetration, Grievious Wound is the important effect for counter the situation.

If bought Blade of the Ruined King and there is none or less regen, healer, or lifesteal champions, take Serylda's Grudge.

If you having trouble with attack speed champion, Frozen Heart can protect you and slowing the enemies attack speed when you attack them. It also grants you Ability Haste to help you reduce your cooldown abilities.

To counter shield champions or items, just buy Serpent's Fang for ez life.

If you feeling concern about bounty on your head, Guardian Angel will save you from it. It has an AD stat that still will help you increase you damage.

After your Guardian Angel poped out, you can sell it and trade it into other items. Bloodthirster can be the alternative item while you waiting the cooldown of Guardian Angel. It has AD stat and A BIG AMOUNT OF PHYSICAL VAMP. If you overcap healed by it, you will get a shield that absorbing damage up into 320 damage (based on level).

In-game Strategy

Laning-Early Game

Depends on the rune that you used (especially Domination rune) and matchup, Ezreal can be pretty weak in the laning phase. If your support aggressive type like Leona, Thresh, and Nautilus, you can join the trade after your support landed the crowd control. When that happen, use your W Essence Flux + E Arcane Shift to take a close position and then if there's no minion at front of you, use Mystic Shot and AA. When you reach level 5, you can start your combo with your ultimate Trueshot Barrage in the bush so the enemy wont react it quickly. If your support other than that, just farm with your Q Mystic Shot and do some poke damage to the enemy. You can bait your enemy with the W Essence Flux + E Arcane Shift once the enemy wasted their ability. Remember, SURVIVING IN THE LANING PHASE IS IMPORTANT.

The thing you should remember is ALWAYS PUSH THE LANE WHENEVER YOU NEED TO RECALL (1 wave = 1 kill). If you missed, the enemy will push your lane to the allies tower and you will miss some last hit and that means you delayed your items timing. Other thing is "It's okay to use your AA often if there's a Minion blocking you." since AA is a guarantee hit and your Q Mystic Shot need an aim first.

Everytime your teammate pinging a missing enemy, be aware of it. It can be the enemy mid laner or jungler that will gank into your lane. Keep an eye for a bit at your map because macro is the thing that will save you everytime.

For the usage of your ability, never do tap unless you close to your target. Aim it further whenever the target want to go. You can also combo your ability with your flash for a guarantee hit especially for your ultimate Trueshot Barrage.

For most of the time, don't waste your W Essnce Flux and E Arcane Shift. W Essence Flux is your source for extra damage that can burst the enemy into a potential kill moment. E Arcane Shift is your escape tools after Flash that will save your since Ezreal is squishy.

When the first dragon spawned while there is a wave in the lane and the enemy haven't come to the river, shove the wave so the enemy tower could get shred as a small punish strategy. If the enemy wave pushed to your lane instead and the enemy have came to the river or pit, leave it and start a fight with your team. In this case, never try to jump ahead or take a position inside the pit since it will be a bad position even you have a flash or another E Arcane Shift. If you at the pit, you will get burst by the enemy and die. Take a furthest poisition between you and your enemy so you won't get jump by the enemy assassin or mage. Also, try not to always stick with your teammate since their also can be the target or a bridge to reach into you.

Mid Game (Winning Condition)

After got your Manamune, clear your lane if you haven't destroy the enemy tower. You can just keep farm and join the fight if it's close to you. You need to get your Trinity Force for damage spike. Once you get it, try to slow push by killing the melee and range Minion and leave the siege last. If there's any a fight, try to join since even you get some assist, gold still a gold. You also need to get your third item as fast as possible for your power spike. Usually, your third item would be a counter items but when you too ahead from foes, just get Serylda's Grudge or Riftmaker for guarantee kill.

For positioning, same as at early game before, try not to close with your teammate especially when you have bounty on your head because everyone will try to hunt you down and take the reward from you. Once you dead, there is no other dps in the team (if your team sucks). Never try to jump into crowd unless there is your front liner in there. Sometimes, even it's there, I wouldn't recommend that act since you're an ADC and you're the priority target for your enemies.

Mid Game (Losing Condition)

First, I would say the important thing. DON'T BE MAD IF YOU LOSE YOUR LANE AND THE GAME IS NOT ENDED YET! Mentality is the key to carry your teammate and even yourself to the goal. In this case, you should farm more often like taking jungle camp, clearing the lane, and even igonring a fight if there's not a guaranted kill in it. Your goal in this time is to get your Trinity Force as quick as possible. With it, you will able to join the fight and secure some kill that potentially will bring you to the next item in short times. If there any non-escape champions in the enemy line, kill first for your guarantee gold if it's possible. Always do lock target and use that feature because it helps you a lot for targeting especially when you ran out of ability.

Late Game (Winning Condition)

Keep playing for objective especially for tower. At late game, you can farm more less since you have to end the game before going to more late. Ezreal is pretty weak in the late game because everyone could catch up the net worth if they're succeed on picking off enemy.

Tank can be more tanky, carry can be more aggressive, bruiser can rush you down, assassin or mage can one shot you. Overall, late game is a difficult situation if you not end the game quickly.

Offensive warding can also be useful because you can do a better fight since you and your whole team can see the enemy ability that used.

Late Game (Losing Condition)

This is a difficult situation for Ezreal. Whenever he's behind, it's hard to deal a decent damage esepecially into tanky enemy. Just like mid game situation, keep farm until your counter items is ready and just do some amount of poking damage and hopefully you and your teammate can execute the enemy. Keep split pushing for a while and go back if there is no vision or enemy on the map. Ward more often to increase your awarness especially when you walk passing river.
